Archetype (n): The original pattern or model from which all things of the same kind are copied or on which they are based.

Many attempts have been made to provide patterns, models and frameworks for "How the work works". Extracting their respective documentation and manifestos into the five levels of a spine, these can be mapped using the Spine Model. This makes them easier to understand and to start to compare to each other. It also makes them easier to use as a departure point and effectively evolve to suit your specific context.
